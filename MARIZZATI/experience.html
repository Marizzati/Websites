WORK EXPERIENCE




Majlis Perbandaran Bentong, Pahang

VIEW

On April 2021 until August 2021, I had my internship for diploma at Majlis Perbandaran Bentong, Pahang (MPB). In the picture below show the front building for MPB. I was placed in the management department where I gained so much knowledge and experience. I was given the opportunity to do a lot of work, including recording files,taking complaints, answering calls from outsiders and staff, and I learned a lot more.

Click the "view" button where it contains a picture of my last day internship in MPB and I am suprised because they made a farewell party for me. I am so grateful surrounded with good people there, they are so helpful and understanding when they know that Im still learning during the internship. Besides that, they also gave me some presents and in the picture above was my supervisor. She was very kind and helpful and for me had internship here such a great experience.





Watsons Bentong, Pahang

VIEW

In the beginning of November 2021, I started my first job as a Customer Assistant at Watsons Bentong, Pahang. It was a great experience for working at retail store. Im so glad that Watsons Bentong's Team was very helpful and easy-going. Working in retail store were not easy actually but Im proud that I survived with the team. Plus, I learned and did so many things such as stock take, arranged stocks in planogram and being a cashier.

At the end of February, I had an opportunity to continue my degree and planned to quit from the job. 3 days before I quit, Bentong's Team had a party for me which they brought some food from Marybrown and we had them together. I am so blessed to worked as a family with them. Besides that, with an open heart, I still want to work with them and decided to do a part time job there during my semester break. Lastly, I inserted a picture in the "view" button of me and my Bentong's Team during my last shift.
